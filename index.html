Writer Mechanics Suite · v1.3
Seed Engineer · Focus & Mechanics Tools

A tiny offline suite for serious drafts: one focus-first writing space plus ten diagnostic tools for mechanics, fiction depth, and misanthropic niche spice.

Go way beyond basic grammar checking. Dig into exactly how to perfect your writing with reports that check for overused words, tired clichés, awkward phrasings, and more.
