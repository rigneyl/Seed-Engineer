Mitigation paths:

Require a ‚ÄúFounders Declaration‚Äù confirming clear IP rights before listing.

üåç Risk: Cross-Jurisdiction Compliance

Users may trade micro-stakes across borders ‚Äî each country has its own financial, tax, and securities regulations.

üîÑ Risk: Unsustainable Economics

‚öôÔ∏è 3. OPERATIONAL & PLATFORM RISKS üß± Risk: Data Integrity

Tracking fractional ownership requires robust data models ‚Äî errors or lost data could trigger disputes.
